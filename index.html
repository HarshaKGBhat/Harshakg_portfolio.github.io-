What I Do

I believe ideas deserve to fly — not lie on paper.

I’ve always had the urge to not just observe ideas, but to shape them into something real. I believe ideas written on paper deserve to be folded and tested just like a paper plane, whether they fly high or not. For me, creation matters more than leaving imagination untouched.

My work lives in the space where gaps in systems meet solutions through AI, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Robotics.

Growing up in a creative environment nurtured my curiosity and taught me to think differently, to question and to build. I still carry that mindset today: every learning I gain, I try to translate into something practical, whether it’s a research paper, an internship project, or an experiment of my own. Experience is valuable, but applying that experience with relevance makes the real difference.

Who I Am

I am Harsha Keladi Ganapathi, originally from Keladi village in Sagara taluk, Karnataka, India. However, I was brought up in Mysuru, Karnataka. My journey blends cultural roots, agrarian background, academic curiosity, and passion for sports and teaching. Currently, I’m pursuing my Master of Science in Data Science at the University of New Haven, CT, USA.
