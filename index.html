What I Do

Dedicated to the transformative power of data science, my journey began in high school, where a shift from disliking mathematical subjects to embracing the world of data analysis occurred. This evolution culminated in my leadership role in creating the "Areca Nut Tree Climbing and Pesticide Spraying Machine Using Android," securing the first prize at the 7th National Level IEEE Project Expo in 2023. As an Information Science Engineering graduate, my commitment to data science remained steadfast. Seeking to expand my knowledge, I pursued certification courses in Python programming, data analytics, and Tableau during my undergraduate studies. Interning in machine learning at Take It Smart OPC Pvt Ltd and interning in Data science at Solar Secure Solutions which lead into projects like "Machine Learning-Based Customer Segmentation" ,"Kannada Poets Image Classification" and "Hotel Booking Predictions" enriched my practical skills. My aspirations extend beyond personal growth; I actively participate in social initiatives, such as Swachh Bharat, digital awareness programs, and tutoring in rural villages. In sports, I served as the captain of college teams, leading them to consecutive championships. Driven by a passion for social impact, I actively engage in various activities, embodying leadership in sports and contributing to societal well-being. This multifaceted approach reflects my commitment to academic excellence, personal growth, and making a positive difference in the world.
